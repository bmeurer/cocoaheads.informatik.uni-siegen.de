About CocoaHeads

CocoaHeads.org is an international group devoted to discussion of Apple's Cocoa and CocoaTouch frameworks for programming on Mac OS X and iOS. During monthly meetings, members present on their projects and offer tutorials on various programming topics.

We are the CocoaHeads Siegen Chapter, hosted by the Lehrstuhl für Compilerbau und Softwareanalyse at the Universität Siegen. We get together for informal short talks and presentations, and to talk about programming.
